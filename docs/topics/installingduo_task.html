How to Enable Duo for the First Time

Duo Security multi-factor authentication is available and required for all UMN students, staff, and faculty anywhere a UMN internet ID is used.

Note: You must add your devices to Duo before use. While you can remove your device later, you cannot opt out after opting in to the service.

  1. Sign into my-account.umn.edu with your UMN Internet ID and password.
  2. Select Duo Security from the navigation menu on the left-hand side of the page.
  3. In the drop-down menu, choose Enable Duo Security.
  4. Select Yes to opt in and click Submit.
