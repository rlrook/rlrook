How to Add Devices to Duo

Any device added can be removed from Duo at your discretion.

We strongly recommend adding at least two devices to your Duo Security multi-factor authentication account in case you cannot access your primary device for any reason.

  1. Sign into my-account.umn.edu with your UMN Internet ID and password.
  2. If you have a device already added to Duo, authenticate using bypass code, Duo push, or a call.
  3. If you do not have a backup device or bypass codes AND your new device has a different phone number, prepare a valid form of photo ID and contact Technology Help for assistance.
  4. Select Duo Security from the navigation menu.
  5. Select Create or Manage Duo Account.
  6. Authenticate with Duo a second time using either step 2 or 3.
  7. Select Add another device.
  8. Select the type of device you wish to add from the options: mobile phone, tablet, landline, security key, or touch ID. If you wish to enroll a smartphone tied to an international (non-US) phone number, add the device as a Tablet rather than a Mobile phone.
  9. Click Continue and follow the prompts to complete enrollment for your device.
  10. If you are adding a smart phone or a tablet, you will be prompted to scan a QR code or use an activation link to activate Duo mobile. If the QR code can't be scanned, select Email me an activation link instead.
  11. Once you have scanned the QR code or followed the emailed link on the device you want to add, select Continue to complete the enrollment process.
