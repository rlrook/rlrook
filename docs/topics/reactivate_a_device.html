Reactivate A Device

If you are replacing a phone that is already associated with your Duo Security account, follow the steps below.

Remember to activate your new device while you are in possession of both the old and new devices.

  1. Sign into my-account.umn.edu with your UMN Internet ID and password.
  2. Authenticate with Duo using your backup device, a bypass code, or prepare a valid form of photo ID and contact Technology Help for assistance.
  3. Select Duo Security from the navigation menu.
  4. Select Create or Manage Duo Account.
  5. Authenticate with Duo a second time using one of the options from step 2.
  6. Next to the device you are reactivating or replacing, select Device Options.
  7. Select Reactivate Duo Mobile.
  8. If you are reactivating an existing phone number on your account, you will be prompted to verify ownership of the device by clicking Call Me and entering the six digit verification code.
  9. You will be prompted to install Duo Mobile on your device. Once the Duo Mobile application is installed on your device, select I have Duo Mobile Installed.
  10. If you are adding a smart phone or a tablet, you will be prompted to scan a QR code or use an activation link to activate Duo mobile by selecting the email option.
  11. Once you have scanned the QR code or followed the emailed link on the device you are reactivating, select Continue to complete the enrollment process.
